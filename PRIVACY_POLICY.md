Privacy Policy for BooTube

Last updated: 2026-06-13

## What Data We Collect
BooTube collects no personal data, browsing history, or metrics. The only data collected is the list of censored words you manually add to your blocklist and your basic UI toggle preferences (e.g., whether the Blur effect is enabled).

## How Data Is Stored
Your custom blocklist and extension preferences are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`. 

## How Data Is Used
Your blocklist is used exclusively to scan the local YouTube closed-caption text to determine when to mute the video player.

## Third-Party Services
This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics trackers, or external APIs.

## Data Sharing
BooTube does NOT transmit any data off your device. Your blocklist and preferences are never shared with the developer, Google, or any third party.

## Data Retention and Deletion
Data is retained locally on your machine for as long as the extension is installed. You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the extension or removing words from your blocklist menu.

## Changes to This Policy
We will update this policy if our data practices change.

## Contact
For privacy inquiries, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support page.
