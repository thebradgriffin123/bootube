Troubleshooting FAQ

Why isn't muting/censoring working on Plex, Disney+, or Hulu? ▼

Our filter engine relies on reading subtitle data to determine timing. Subtitles must be turned on in the media player interface (e.g. English [CC]) for censoring to function correctly.

Can I adjust the length of the audio mute? ▼

Yes! Tapping on the BooTube toolbar icon opens your settings menu. Under "Advanced settings", you can use the **Mute buffer slider** to adjust the safety cushioning (from tight 0.3s up to a wide 1.0s cushion).

Is my search history or data collected? ▼

No. BooTube is built with a privacy-first mindset. All subtitle interception, parsing, and muting calculation runs 100% locally on your browser. Your data is never sent to external servers.

How do I copy a specific swear word into my list? ▼

For the best results, copy the word exactly as it appears in the video's Closed Captions, including any special punctuation (like trailing periods or dashes) that might be attached to the word.

Can I whitelist specific channels? ▼

Yes! If Respectful Mode's auto-detect fails to whitelist a sacred term on a podcast, click the BooTube icon in your toolbar while watching the video and toggle the "Trust Channel" switch to whitelist it.
