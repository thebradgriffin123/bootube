Frequently Asked Questions

Does BooTube work on Netflix or Hulu?

Currently, BooTube is designed exclusively for YouTube. It specifically hooks into YouTube's custom video player and closed captioning system.

Will YouTube ban my account for using this?

No! BooTube does not block ads or violate YouTube's Terms of Service. It simply reads the captions that YouTube already provides and adjusts the volume slider on your computer.

Is my data sold to third parties?

Absolutely not. BooTube operates 100% offline within your browser. We don't have servers, we don't track your viewing habits, and we don't sell your data.

A word is on my blocklist, but it wasn't filtered. Why?

We are always working to make the experience better, but because BooTube relies on closed captions, there are a few edge cases. Sometimes when multiple people are talking, the captions only transcribe the main speaker. Additionally, if the captions attach punctuation directly to the word (like "word."), it can sometimes slip past the exact-match scanner.
